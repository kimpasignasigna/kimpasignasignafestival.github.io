PAHIYAS Festival
DESCRIPTION

Pahiyas is a thanksgiving festival in honor of San Isidro Labrador, the patron saint of farmers. It is known for the decorated houses using agricultural products.

HISTORY

It began as a ritual offering of farm goods to San Isidro. Over time, it evolved into an elaborate festival showing gratitude for a bountiful harvest.
