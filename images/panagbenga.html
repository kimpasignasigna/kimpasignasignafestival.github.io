PANAGBENGA Festival
DESCRIPTION

The Panagbenga Festival, held annually in Baguio City, Philippines, is a vibrant celebration of the city's blooming flowers and cultural heritage. It's a month-long event that showcases the beauty of nature, the resilience of the community, and the creativity of local artists. The festival features flower-covered floats, street dancing competitions, and various cultural performances.

HISTORY

The Panagbenga Festival, Baguio's Flower Festival, began in 1995 as a way to help the city recover from the 1990 Luzon earthquake and boost tourism. The word "Panagbenga" translates to "season of blooming" in Kankanaey, reflecting the city's abundant floral heritage. It has since become Baguio's largest annual event, attracting visitors from around the world.
