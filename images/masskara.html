Masskara Festival
DESCRIPTION

MassKara Festival is a colorful and joyful celebration held every October in Bacolod City, known as the "City of Smiles." The festival is famous for its vibrant street dancing, participants wearing elaborately decorated smiling masks, and festive music that fills the city streets..

HISTORY

The festival began in 1980 during a time of crisis, both economically and emotionally, in Bacolod. The local government initiated the event to uplift the community's spirit after the sugar industry collapsed and a tragic ferry accident occurred, claiming hundreds of lives. The name "MassKara" is a play on the words "mass" (people) and "kara" (face), reflecting unity and resilience through smiling masks.
