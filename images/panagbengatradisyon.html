Dance

The Panagbenga Festival dance is a lively street parade celebrating Baguio and Cordillera culture, featuring performers in flower-themed costumes inspired by the Ibaloi Bendian dance and set to indigenous and modern music.

Cloth

Panagbenga Festival costumes are bright and floral, blending Cordilleran native attire with vibrant designs. Dancers wear embroidered blouses, flowing skirts, and floral accessories, paired with traditional light footwear like bakya or tsinelas.

Music

Panagbenga Festival music is energetic and festive, combining traditional indigenous sounds with modern rhythms. Performed by marching bands and drum and lyre groups, it uplifts both dancers and spectators.
